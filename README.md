# EventVenue.Asia

> Find, compare, and book the perfect venue and services for your event across Southeast Asia.

EventVenue.Asia is an online marketplace that connects you with verified event venues and service providers — caterers, photographers, decorators, entertainment, and more — across Malaysia, Singapore, Thailand, and Indonesia. Whether you're planning a wedding, a corporate function, or an intimate celebration, the platform helps you discover the right space, reach out to vendors, and organise your event from start to finish, with dedicated support for halal-certified vendors.

---

## What You Can Do

### Plan and book your event
- **Search venues** by city, guest capacity, amenities, event type, halal certification, and budget.
- **Compare** up to three venues side by side to weigh price, capacity, and features.
- **Save favourites** to a wishlist so you can come back to them later.
- **Send an inquiry** to a venue with your event date and guest count, then track its progress.
- **Chat on WhatsApp** — once you send an inquiry, the venue can reach you directly on WhatsApp to discuss the details.
- **Read reviews** from real guests and leave your own after your event.

### Get help from the Smart Planner
Describe your event in plain language and let the AI Smart Planner do the legwork. Tell it something like:

> "I need a halal wedding venue in KL for 200 guests next month, with catering and photography, budget RM15,000."

It instantly suggests matching venues and services, ranked for fit, with a budget breakdown so you can see what's realistic.

### Discover services, not just venues
Beyond venues, browse wedding and event services by category — catering, photography, photobooths, ice cream carts, florists, cakes, bridal cars, live bands, makeup, henna, and more. Filter by the type of service you need.

### For venue owners and service providers
- List your venue or service with photos, pricing, and amenities.
- Receive customer inquiries and respond instantly over WhatsApp.
- Manage your availability calendar and keep track of bookings.
- Build trust with a verified badge once your business is approved.

## Getting Started

### As a customer

1. **Visit the site** and start browsing — no account needed to look around.
2. **Create a free account** with your name, email, phone number, and a password. Your phone number is how venues will reach you on WhatsApp.
3. **Search or use the Smart Planner** to find venues and services that fit your event.
4. **Send an inquiry** to any venue you like. The venue will follow up with you directly.

### As a vendor

1. **Register your business** through the vendor sign-up, choosing whether you own a venue or provide a service.
2. **Upload your verification documents** (such as a business licence or halal certificate).
3. **Wait for approval** — our team reviews every vendor to keep the marketplace trustworthy.
4. **Publish your listings** once approved, and start receiving inquiries.

## How It Works

### Booking a venue (step by step)

1. From the homepage, use the search bar or filters to narrow down venues by location, capacity, or amenities.
2. Click a venue to see its photos, amenities, reviews, and available dates.
3. Select **Send Inquiry**, then enter your event date, time, guest count, and any special requirements.
4. Submit — your inquiry goes straight to the venue, and they'll reach out to you on WhatsApp to continue the conversation.
5. Follow the status of all your inquiries from your dashboard.

### Using the Smart Planner

1. On the homepage, switch to the **EventVenue Smart Planner** tab.
2. Type a description of your event in everyday language, including details like guest count, location, budget, and the services you need.
3. Review the recommended venues and services, each scored for how well it matches, along with an estimated budget.
4. Send an inquiry to any recommendation that catches your eye.

### Comparing venues

1. Open the **Compare** page.
2. Add up to three venues.
3. View them side by side — price, capacity, rating, halal certification, amenities, and event types — to make a confident choice.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need an account to browse?**
No. You can explore venues and services freely. You'll need a free account to save favourites and send inquiries.

**How will a venue contact me?**
After you send an inquiry, the venue reaches out to you directly on WhatsApp using the phone number on your account — so keep it accurate.

**Is there any charge to send an inquiry?**
No. Sending an inquiry is free and doesn't commit you to anything. The venue will follow up with details and pricing.

**Can I plan my event without knowing exactly what I want?**
Yes. The Smart Planner is designed for exactly that — describe your event in plain words and it will suggest options that fit.
